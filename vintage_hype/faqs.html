FAQs

What is Vintage Hype?

Vintage Hype is a vintage clothing store that sells vintage and streetwear clothing located in Alhambra, CA. Our physical store opened during the heart of the pandemic, and understand the struggles and hardships COVID-19 has impacted all of us. In addition as a business we value community and are always ensuring that we work together rather than competing with one another.

How long does my order take to ship?

As soon as we get confirmation of your order we will ship your order within 2 business days.

When will you restock your inventory?

We do a monthly restock of our inventory in-store and on our online store. To stay up to date with when we restock, follow us on instagram!

Where do you get your supplies from?

We source our supplies and clothing from different suppliers located all over Los Angeles. As we grow to expand our company we are always looking for more suppliers from all over the country.

Return/Exchange Policy:

We currently do not accept returns or exchanges. We apologize for the inconvenience! However, if you are completely dissatisfied with our product, we urge you to reach out to us via Instagram direct messages or through e-mail. A crew member will get back to you and we will work things out on a case-by-case basis. We appreciate your patience and grace!
